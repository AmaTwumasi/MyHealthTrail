MISSION

Our mission is to make quality healthcare easily accessible to expectant and postpartum moms right on thier digital devices. In alignment with the third goal of SDGs we aim to contribute to the reduction in healthcare wait time, lack of access to personal health records and ultimately the high rate of maternal mortality by providing a platform of verfied, carefully selected local and international doctors and dieticians/nutritionists for expactant and postpartum moms to get fast medical and dietary consultations and second opinions
